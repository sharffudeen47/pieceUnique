Pièce Unique

Exclusive Boho Chic Design Furniture in Bruges

Pièce Unique curates and creates boho chic furniture that blends modern lines with artisanal craftsmanship from around the world. We design in Bruges, collaborate with skilled partners, and bring each treasure directly to you.

Harmoniously
Merging Worlds

We let the culture of here merge with the culture of elsewhere—our native Morocco. Our life experiences shape our designs: childhood in Marrakesh’s stylish design environment, arriving in Belgium, and embracing its precision. Our training in graphic design, interior design, and photography, plus fashion-world experience, all come together. Beauty and design come first in everything we do.

Fairplay on Fairpay

Our pieces fit antique and modern interiors—exclusive yet accessible. For us, business means fair play and fair pay. We benefit from our work, and we make sure partners benefit, too. We know everyone we work with personally. That matters.
